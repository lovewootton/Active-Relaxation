Levin Trig Walkway
Walk, Run, Ride, Escape!

This popular spot is situated on private land and features walking and biking tracks and access to Tararua Forest Park via Kohitere Forest.

The Kohitere Forest, Trig Walk, link track to the Te Araroa Trail, and Mountain Bike tracks. From the top of the Trig Walk you can enjoy a panoramic view across the entire Horowhenua and beyond to Kapiti Island to the South and Mt Ruapehu to the North.

The track connects to the Mangahao Makahika track leading north to Palmerston North and is part of the Te Araroa trail.

Members of the Public who wish to access the Kohitere Forest are reminded that you are entering private property and are privileged to do so. Please observe the following regulations to ensure this recreational resource remains available for public access.

Waitarere forest walk

Enjoy this 5.6-km circular trail near Levin, Manawatu-Wanganui. Generally considered an easy route, it takes an average of 1 h 8 min to complete. This trail is great for hiking, running, and walking, and it's unlikely you'll encounter many other people while exploring.
